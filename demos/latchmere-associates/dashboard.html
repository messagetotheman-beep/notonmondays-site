Project overview

Client
Latchmere & Associates, Solicitors

A clear, accessible website for people navigating stressful legal situations — built in plain English, designed around user needs
Approach
GOV.UK Service Manual principles, WCAG 2.2 AA, mobile-first
Key constraint
Many users may be in distress, have English as a second language, or be unfamiliar with legal terminology
