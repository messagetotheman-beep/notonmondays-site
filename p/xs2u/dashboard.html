What we need from you for products

For each product you want to sell, we need the following information. The more complete this is, the faster we can go live.

Field What it is Required
Product name The name of the item as it appears on the site Must have
Category Women, Men, or Accessories Must have
Price Selling price in GBP Must have
Sizes available e.g. XS, S, M, L, XL or numerical sizing Must have
Photos At least one clear image per product, plain background preferred Must have
Short description 2–4 sentences about the item. Material, fit, key details. Must have
Stock quantity How many of each size you have available Must have
Colours If the product comes in multiple colours Good to have
Size guide Measurements or a guide to help customers choose the right size Good to have
Compare-at price Original price if the item is on sale Optional

Next steps

  • 1
    Send product information Use the table above as a guide. Photos and product names are the priority.
  • 2
    Review trust content We'll share the delivery, returns, and about copy for you to check before it goes live.
  • 3
    Confirm Shopify Payments You'll need to verify your business details in Shopify. Takes around 10 minutes.
  • 4
    Test and launch We'll run through the full checkout together to make sure everything works before going live.
