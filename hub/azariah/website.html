Our purpose

The NHS is under pressure to do more with less. We help with the systems part.

NHS England's 2025/26 guidance requires trusts to reduce costs by at least 1% and improve productivity by 4%. Workforce compliance done manually works against both goals. Azariah automates the checks, reduces the admin, and gives your team visibility without the overhead.

We enable the people who show up to care, by improving the systems that support them, so those who need help receive it when it matters most.

Workforce verification

Automated checks on credentials, right-to-work, DBS, and professional registrations including NMC, GMC, and HCPC. Stop chasing paperwork. Start having confidence in every person on your books.

NHS framework compliance

Built around NHS Workforce Alliance and Health Trust Europe framework requirements. Stay audit-ready across your entire workforce without the manual tracking, missed renewals, or last-minute scrambles.

Faster time to deployment

Trusts under pressure to fill vacancies quickly need compliance processes that keep pace. Azariah reduces the time between identifying a worker and deploying them safely, without cutting corners.

Workforce oversight

As the NHS moves towards the 2026/27 Oversight Framework, organisations need clear visibility of workforce health metrics. Azariah gives managers real-time insight, not quarterly reports built from spreadsheets.

About us

A team that knows what's at stake

Azariah was built by people who understand the operational realities of UK healthcare. Not from the outside, but from having worked within and alongside NHS systems where compliance failures have real consequences.

The NHS is at a turning point. The 10-Year Workforce Plan, the shift from analogue to digital, new oversight frameworks, and the ESR system transition are creating both pressure and opportunity. Organisations that build the right infrastructure now will be better placed on the other side of that change.

We are not a consultancy, and we are not trying to sell you a complex platform. We build focused, practical systems that solve specific problems. We measure ourselves on whether they actually make your team's work easier.

We are based in the UK and work exclusively within the British healthcare market. We are early stage, which means you get direct access to the people building the product.
