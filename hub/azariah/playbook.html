Vision

A principle that grounds every decision we make.

"

We enable the people who show up to care, by improving the systems that support them, so those who need help receive it when it matters most.

This is not a tagline. It is a principle that should be grounded in every decision we make — what we build, who we partner with, and how we work.

05 — Weekly Rhythm
05

Weekly Rhythm

A simple weekly cycle to create clarity and momentum without overhead.

Start of week 30 to 45 mins
Sprint Planning
Align on what matters
  • What are we trying to achieve this week?
  • What are the top 3 to 5 priorities?
  • Who owns each item?
Output Clear weekly goal and agreed priorities
Midweek · Twice 15 mins
Standup
Stay on track, unblock fast
  • What am I working on?
  • Am I blocked?
  • What's next?
Note No deep dives. Take those offline.
End of week 45 to 60 mins
Review, Demo and Reflect
Learn, improve, show progress
  • Show and Tell: what have we shipped and what value does it deliver?
  • Reflect: what worked, what didn't, what should change?
  • Wrap-up: what's carrying over? Any blockers going into next week?
Output Visibility, learning, and continuous improvement

06 — Standards
06

Standards

What good looks like. Two lists worth keeping visible.

We aim for
  • ✓ Clear weekly goal
  • ✓ 3 to 5 focused priorities
  • ✓ Work visible to everyone
  • ✓ Fast unblocking
  • ✓ Regular demos of progress
  • ✓ Continuous improvement every week
We avoid
  • ✕ Big, unclear tasks
  • ✕ Hidden work
  • ✕ Overloaded meetings
  • ✕ Silent blockers
  • ✕ Building without user value

This is intentionally lightweight. Try it. Learn from it. Improve it together.
The goal is not process. The goal is better outcomes for real people.
