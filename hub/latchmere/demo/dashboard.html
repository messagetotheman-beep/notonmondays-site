Project overview

Client
Latchmere & Associates, Solicitors
Brief
A clear, accessible website for people navigating stressful legal situations. Plain English, structured around user needs, not legal categories.
Approach
GOV.UK Service Manual principles. WCAG 2.2 AA. Mobile-first.
Constraints
Users may be in distress, have English as a second language, or have no familiarity with legal terminology. Design must not assume any of these.
