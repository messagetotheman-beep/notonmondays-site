Operational support for law firms
and legal tech teams.

We apply proven operational and systems capability to the kinds of delivery and systems challenges law firms face, improving how work flows, how systems connect, and how change gets delivered.

Start a conversation

Where firms get stuck

Common operational challenges.

Work is fragmented

Tasks rely on individual knowledge rather than consistent systems. When people leave, the process goes with them.

Systems do not connect

Case management, CRM, and billing tools operate in isolation. Staff work around gaps rather than through joined-up processes.

Fee earners absorb overhead

Time that should go to clients goes to chasing information or navigating systems that do not match how work actually runs.

Change does not land

New systems get introduced without the delivery structure to make them stick. Good intentions produce short-term change that fades.

How we help

What we can work on together.

We start by understanding how work actually moves through your firm, where it slows down, where it duplicates, and where people are working around broken processes. We map the real flow, not the intended one, and identify where targeted improvements would have the most impact.

This can be a standalone diagnostic or the starting point for a longer engagement.

We design and build websites for law firms that help clients understand what the firm does and how to get in touch. Sites are accessible, clearly structured, and written in plain language.

We also support broader digital service improvement for client-facing or internal processes, designed around what users actually need to do.

We help firms get more from the systems they already have, or select and implement the right ones. This includes CRM platforms such as Salesforce, particularly where systems need to better support how work actually happens.

We are not a software provider. We work on your side: helping you decide what to buy, configuring it around how your team works, and making sure people use it.

Most change fails not because the plan is wrong but because delivery is not managed properly. We stay involved through implementation, removing blockers, tracking progress, and adjusting when things do not go as expected.

We can lead a change programme end to end, or come in to steady one that has already started.
