Our Story

We built this because ambitious businesses deserved better.

Not On Mondays exists because the best people we've ever worked with had one thing in common: extraordinary at their craft, clear on their vision, and consistently underserved by everyone brought in to help them.

  • ◆Consultants who delivered decks and disappeared.

We're none of those things.

AI Advisory

Not AI-curious.
AI-serious.

AI is changing what good operations looks like. We're not here to tell you it's coming. We're already using it, building with it, and helping the businesses we work with make sense of it: what it means for their strategy, their teams, and their legal and ethical obligations.

Think of it less like a technology upgrade and more like the shift when the internet changed how businesses communicated. The question isn't whether to engage with it. It's how, when, and on whose terms. We want to be part of that journey with you: as practitioners who've done the work, not consultants selling the idea.

AI Strategy & Leadership Advisory
Senior-level guidance on where AI fits in your business model, your team structure, and your competitive positioning. No jargon. No fluff.
AI Integration into Operations
We identify where AI can replace, augment, or accelerate your existing workflows: then we help you actually implement it, not just plan it.
Team AI Workshops & Training
Practical, hands-on sessions that bring your team from AI-anxious to AI-capable. Built around your tools, your workflows, your reality.
AI Ethics, Compliance & Governance
The legal and ethical landscape around AI is moving fast. We help you understand your obligations, build responsible practices into how your teams use AI, and stay on the right side of emerging regulation.
